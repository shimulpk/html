Bangladesh Cricket

Cricket was introduced to Bangladesh centuries ago but gained prominence after the country's independence in 1971. The Bangladesh Cricket Control Board was established in 1972,and the team became an Associate Member of the ICC in 1977. Key milestones include winning the 1997 ICC Trophy, qualifying for their first World Cup in 1999, and achieving full ICC membership in 2000, becoming the tenth Test-playing nation.
